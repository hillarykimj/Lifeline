Lifeline was created by Manpreet, Rumesh, Mayesha and Hillary to help educate everyday people on first aid.

Why is learning First Aid so important ?

  1. You can help save a loved ones life - did you know 80% of cardiac arrests occur in the home
  2. You will have improved confidence to respond in emergancies
  3. Ambulances take an estimated 8 minutes to arrive at an incident you will need to respond quickly as times is not on your side

Experts say you need to be retrain on First Aid every 6 months
